Our Products

Quartz
Quartz is a hard, crystalline mineral that is commonly used in the production of glass, ceramics, electronics, and many other products. It is also used as a component in the manufacturing of quartz countertops, which have become increasingly popular in modern homes.
READ MORE
Feldspar
Feldspar is a group of rock-forming minerals that are commonly used in ceramics, glassmaking, and as a filler in paints, plastics, and other products. It is also used as a flux in the production of glass and ceramics, which helps to reduce the melting point of the raw materials.
READ MORE
Ramming Mass
Ramming mass is a refractory material that is used in the lining of furnaces and kilns to protect them from high temperatures and thermal shock. It is typically made from a combination of quartz, feldspar, and other minerals, and is designed to withstand the harsh conditions of industrial processes.
READ MORE
Dolomite
Dolomite, a mineral composed of calcium magnesium carbonate, is utilized extensively in various industries due to its versatility and abundance. Common applications include construction materials, such as concrete and asphalt, as well as in agriculture to neutralize soil acidity. However, dolomite mining can pose environmental challenges, including habitat disruption and water pollution.
READ MORE
Silica Sand
Silica sand, composed of silicon dioxide, is another crucial mineral resource widely used in industries like glass manufacturing, foundries, and hydraulic fracturing for oil and gas extraction. Its high purity and unique properties make it indispensable in numerous industrial processes. However, silica sand mining operations can have significant environmental impacts, including habitat destruction and air and water pollution.
